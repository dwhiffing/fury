NATO/US Navy/US Navy CVBGs

CVBG Eisenhower (CVN-69)

The Eisenhower starts the war on patrol in the Western Mediterranean on a routine patrol with 6th Fleet. After spending three days of intense fighting in the Med, including intensive strikes on Libya, the CVBG is ordered to rapidly reinforce the North Atlantic. The Nimitz Battle Group enters the Eastern Med through the Suez Canal as the Eisenhower departs through Gibraltar on 19 Feb.
